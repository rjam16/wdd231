Art Forms

Digital Painting

Specialized Techniques: Color theory and Layering

Common Software: Krita, Adobe Photoshop, Procreate, Clip Studio Paint

Careers: Concept Artist, Illustrator, Character Artist, Environment Artist, Graphic Designers, Storyboard Artist, Matte Painter

Vector Art

Specialized Techniques: Paths and Anchors, Color and Gradients, Typography

Common Software: Adobe Illustrator, Inkscape, Affinity Designer

Careers: UI/UX Designer, Graphic Designers, Environment Artists, Illustrators

Animation: 2D or 3D

Specialized Techinques: Mesh Modeling, Materials and Textures, Lighting and Rendering, Animation and Rigging

Common Software: Pencil2D, Toon Boom, Adobe Animate CC, Blender, Autodesk Maya, Cinema 4D

Careers: Animator, VFX Artists, 3D Modeler

Pixel Art

Specialized Techniques: Color Palettes, Grids and Zoom, Anti-Aliasing and Dithering

Common Software: Aesprite, Pixquare, Piskel

Careers: Concept Artist, Illustrator, Character Artist, Environment Artist, Graphic Designers, UI/UX Designer

Fractal Art

Specialized Techniques: Self-Similarity, Psychedelia, Intricate Patterns, Mathematical Patterns in Nature, Vivid Colors

Common Software: Mandelbulb 3D, Apophysis, Frax

Careers: Illustrator, Animator, VFX Artist, Environment Artist

3D Modeling & Sculpting

Specialized Techniques: Brush-based Interface, Dynamesh and Retopology, Detailing and Refinement, Polypainting and Texture Painting

Common Software: ZBrush, Mudbox, Blender, Autodesk Maya, Pencil2D Animation

Careers: Character Artist, Environment Artist, 3D Modeler, VFX Artist, Animator

Digital Collage

Specialized Techniques: Layers and Masks, Adjustment Layers, Selection Tools, Retouching and Healing

Common Software: Adobe Photoshop, Core Painter, Procreate, Kapwing Collage Maker, Canva

Careers: Illustrator, Graphic Designer
